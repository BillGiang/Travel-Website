Why visit Tokyo?

Tokyo is home to wonderful and unique cultures! From their food, language, and lifestyle, there is so much cultural explortion to do! Tokyo is actively evolving at an exponential rate! Their technology is evolving, their systems are evolving, but most importantly, their architectures are evolving! Tokto isn't filled with just man-made wonders though, their natural landscape and greenscape is just as beautiful!
